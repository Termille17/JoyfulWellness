Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Joyful Wellness provide?

We offer holistic therapeutic services including individual therapy, couples counseling, wellness coaching, and guided workshops for emotional and physical wellbeing.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment using our online form on the homepage or by contacting us directly via phone or email. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance.

What is your cancellation policy?

We kindly ask that you cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before your appointment. Late cancellations may result in a fee.

Do you accept medical aid or insurance?

At this time, our services are private-pay only. We can, however, provide documentation for potential reimbursement.

Is my personal information kept confidential?

Absolutely. All personal and health information is handled with strict confidentiality in line with our Privacy Policy.

Are your counselors qualified?

Yes. All our practitioners are certified professionals with the necessary academic qualifications, registrations, and clinical experience.

Can I attend sessions online?

Yes, we offer both in-person and virtual sessions to meet your convenience and comfort.

What age groups do you work with?

We work with adults, young adults, and teenagers aged 16 and up. We can refer younger clients to specialists.

How often should I attend sessions?

This depends on your goals and needs. We typically start with weekly sessions and adjust accordingly.
