Protecting Your Privacy Every Step of the Way

At Joyful Wellness, your trust is our highest priority. We are committed to safeguarding your personal information with integrity, transparency, and care.

Privacy Policy

At Joyful Wellness, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you engage with our services or visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Full name and contact details (email, phone number, address)
  • Booking and appointment history
  • Health and wellness information you voluntarily provide during sessions
  • Usage data from our website, including IP address and browser information

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for purposes such as:

  • Providing and managing therapy sessions or support services
  • Responding to your queries and bookings
  • Improving our services and website user experience
  • Sending relevant updates and wellness content (with your consent)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Your information may only be shared with our licensed wellness professionals or trusted partners as needed to deliver our services—always with confidentiality in mind.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. You may also opt out of marketing communication by contacting us directly.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All changes will be posted on this page with the latest effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy or this policy, please contact us at mamellom2@gmail.com or call +27 79 497 8060 and view our Terms & Conditions and FAQs
